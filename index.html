FAQ

Clear answers before you download.

What does Retryn track? +
Retryn tracks packages, return windows, refunds, receipts, claim records, and warranty coverage in one dashboard.
Do I need to forward order emails? +
No. Retryn is designed to help you add and manage purchases directly, so setup stays simple and fast.

Free is meant to help users try the core workflow. Pro unlocks unlimited tracking, the vault, premium scanning, export tools, and widget support.
